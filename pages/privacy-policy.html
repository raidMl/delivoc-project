Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy aims for explaining how Delivoc uses and protects the data provided by anyone visiting and/or using Delivoc Website and Delivoc Smart App (collectively referred to as “Delivoc Platform”). In this Policy, any use of the word “you” shall be addressed to any visitor to Delivoc Platform or any user thereof, whereas any use of the words “we” or “us” shall refer to Delivoc. In accordance with Delivoc’s security-oriented service mentality, Delivoc adopts the below-mentioned principles to ensure the confidentiality of the information you provide through Delivoc Platform:

SAFEGUARDING AND SECURING THE DATA

To ensure the security of information uploaded or entered on Delivoc Platform, we keep our systems and technology infrastructure at the most practicable secure level and take necessary precautions to prevent data theft, unauthorized access, or unauthorized disclosure. Entry of information on Delivoc Platform is only intended for enabling the Services offered, commissioned and/or performed through Delivoc Platform or in connection therewith. Delivoc will not lease, sell or distribute your personal information to any third parties, be it a natural or legal person, without the prior consent of the concerned Users or unless required by the law.

LIMITED AUTHORIZED DISCLOSURE

Visitors and other Users cannot display the information disclosed by our Users on Delivoc Platform unless in the very specific and very limited cases relating to the Service and on need-to-know basis (for details please see the Terms & Conditions). Unauthorized persons, including Delivoc staff, cannot access the information shared by Users. Delivoc may disclose such information only within the frame of the relevant authorizations and the applicable laws and regulations. If and when any of the regulatory bodies and/or legislative or executive authorities and bodies having jurisdiction on Delivoc requests disclosure of User information, Delivoc will reveal and disclose such information only within the relevant authorizations. If and when Delivoc needs to collaborate with different entities to outsource any support services, it shall ensure that such other companies shall comply with its privacy standards and conditions.

OUR COOKIE POLICY

Once you agree to allow our Delivoc Platform to use cookies, you also agree to use the data it collects regarding your online behavior (analyze traffic, web pages you spend the most time on, and websites you visit). The data we collect by using cookies is used to customize our Delivoc Platform to your needs, as applicable. After we use the data for statistical analysis, the data is completely removed from our systems. Please note that cookies don’t allow us to gain control of your computer or smartphone in any way. They are strictly used to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not so that we can provide a better experience for you. If you want to disable cookies, you can do it by accessing the settings of your internet browser.

Delivoc shall have no responsibility in connection with any acts or omissions of any third parties, and/or with the terms & conditions, privacy policy and other rules applied by such third parties over which Delivoc has no control whatsoever.
