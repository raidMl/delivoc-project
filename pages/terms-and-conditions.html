1. Introduction

1.1 Delivoc is an innovative Customer to Customer (“C2C”) e-commerce solution that facilitates introduction and matchmaking between independent parties over a mail collection and delivery service from one place to another. Delivoc gives the opportunity to a party who wishes to send mail (“Sender”) to meet with a travelling passenger (“Traveler”) and handover to him a parcel for delivery to an identified receiver (“Receiver”) who shall be waiting at the destination of arrival.
