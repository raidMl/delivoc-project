There’s a smarter way to send items.

Tired of paying for services you don’t need? With Delivoc, you only pay for the exact shipping services you require. Our innovative system incorporates the type, weight, distance, and desired transport time into the cost to ensure that you pay the lowest fees possible on every delivery.
